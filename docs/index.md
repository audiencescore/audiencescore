# Audience Score — Documentation

An open, attestation-gated reputation protocol. The normative protocol is
[spec v0.2a](https://github.com/audiencescore/audiencescore/blob/main/spec/SPEC-v0.2a.md):
roles, the attestation ladder, versioned offerings, receipts, invariants,
and rendering v1 score math.

## Pilot Is Live

Pilot deployment, pre-cryptographic-audit. The independent cryptographic review
and per-vertical legal review gates remain open. The pilot ledger may be reset
and receipts re-issued after the audit.
